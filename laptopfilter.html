Given a budget of 50,000 Rs, you'll need to prioritize specific components for a laptop that can handle the demands of a Computer Science curriculum. Here's a breakdown of essential specs:
Opt for: Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 processors. These offer decent performance for general tasks and basic programming.
Aim for: 8GB of RAM. This is the minimum for smooth multitasking and running multiple applications.
A solid-state drive (SSD) over a hard disk drive (HDD). SSDs offer faster boot times, application load times, and overall system responsiveness.
256GB SSD is sufficient for most students, but consider a 512GB SSD for ample storage.
Since you plan on doing Android Development, you should consider minimum of 2GB of graphics card.
